# Limit Upgrades

How to increase rate limits and quotas.

## Self-service upgrades

### Tier upgrade
Upgrade your plan for higher limits:
1. Go to https://cloud.qnsp.cuilabs.io
2. Navigate to Billing > Plan
3. Select higher tier
4. Limits increase immediately

### Add-ons
Enable specific add-ons for targeted increases:

Add-on availability is deployment-specific.

## Request limit increase

For custom limits:

### Via console
1. Go to Settings > Limits
2. Click "Request Increase"
3. Specify needed limits
4. Provide justification

### Via support

Contact support with the specific limits you need and the business justification.

## Enterprise limits

Enterprise tier includes:
- Custom rate limits
- Unlimited quotas
- Dedicated capacity
- Priority support

## Temporary increases

For planned events:
Request temporary increases via the support portal or email.

## Response time

| Request type | Response time |
|--------------|---------------|
| Tier upgrade | Immediate |
| Add-on | Immediate |
| Custom increase | 1-3 business days |
| Enterprise | Custom |
